TA Hours

TA Hours are a chance for you to ask questions, seek advice, or talk about computer science with trained peers who have been in your shoes!

X Block TA Hours are held in the TA Lab, the room behind the CS Lab with the glass door.

At least 3 TAs are always available during every X block. You can usually find some of them in the TA Lab during breaks in the day, but please be respectful of their wishes if they ask you to return during X block hours.

You may always contact your teacher to schedule an appointment in the morning, after school, or during the day.
